Push A
Monday
Chest focus — compounds before fatigue
7 exercises · tap for form video
Chest
1
Flat barbell / machine chest press
Lead compound — go heavy, rest 2–3 min
4 × 6–10 Form
Form guide
Setup
  • Eyes under the bar, feet flat
  • Shoulder blades retracted & depressed
  • Slight arch in lower back
Execution
  • Bar to mid-chest, elbows at ~75°
  • Press in slight arc back to rack
  • Full lockout without losing shoulder position
⚠ Common mistake
Elbows flaring to 90° — stresses the shoulder joint. Keep them slightly tucked.
2
Incline dumbbell press
Upper chest while still fresh
3 × 8–12 Form
Form guide
Setup
  • Bench at 30–45° only
  • Shoulder blades pulled back into pad
Execution
  • Lower to upper chest, elbows at 75°
  • Press up and slightly inward
  • Don't let shoulders roll forward at top
⚠ Common mistake
Bench too high (>45°) — you end up doing a shoulder press, not upper chest.
Shoulders
3
Dumbbell shoulder press
Seated or standing
3 × 10–12 Form
Form guide
Setup
  • Back straight, core braced
  • Dumbbells at ear level, forearms vertical
Execution
  • Press directly overhead to full lockout
  • Don't shrug at the top
  • Lower controlled back to ear level
⚠ Common mistake
Arching the lower back to compensate. Brace your core and keep ribs down every rep.
Triceps
4
Cable overhead tricep extension
Long head stretch — elbows stay forward
3 × 10–12 Form
Form guide
Setup
  • Rope at top, face away, step forward
  • Elbows pointed forward — not flared
  • Slight forward hinge at hips
Execution
  • Extend forearms forward until arms straight
  • Spread rope at the end, squeeze hard
  • Only forearms move — upper arms stay still
⚠ Common mistake
Elbows drifting out and down during the movement. Keep them pointing straight ahead throughout.
Isolation finishers
5
Cable lateral raise
Cable keeps tension at top unlike dumbbells
3 × 12–15 Form
Form guide
Key cues
  • Cable low, hold across body, slight forward lean
  • Lead with elbow, not hand
  • Pinky slightly higher than thumb at top
  • Slow controlled descent
⚠ Common mistake
Swinging the body to get the arm up. Drop the weight and isolate the side delt properly.
6
Pec deck fly
Squeeze at peak contraction — 1 sec hold
3 × 12–15 Form
Form guide
Key cues
  • Seat at chest height, back flat against pad
  • Hug a barrel — squeeze at peak, 1 sec hold
  • Elbows slightly bent throughout
  • Don't go past a mild stretch at the back
⚠ Common mistake
Going too far back — loads the shoulder joint instead of stretching the chest.
7
Cable pushdown (rope)
Spread rope at bottom, elbows pinned to ribs
2 × 12–15 Form
Form guide
Key cues
  • Upper arms locked to sides — don't move
  • Spread rope apart at full extension
  • Return slowly to ~90° only
⚠ Common mistake
Upper arms drifting forward. Lock elbows to ribs and keep them there the entire set.
Progressive overload: Add weight when you hit the top of the rep range for 2 sessions in a row.

Back width — vertical pulling, lat development
6 exercises · tap for form video
Back — width
1
Lat pulldown (wide, overhand)
Lead compound — drive elbows to pockets
4 × 8–10 Form
Form guide
Key cues
  • Depress shoulders first before pulling
  • Drive elbows down and back toward hips
  • Full stretch at the top every rep
  • Lean back 10–15°, not straight up
⚠ Common mistake
Pulling with biceps not lats. Think "elbows to pockets" — biceps are just hooks.
2
Seated cable row (wide grip)
Chest tall, squeeze shoulder blades
3 × 10–12 Form
Form guide
Key cues
  • Torso upright and still — no rocking
  • Retract shoulder blades first, then pull
  • Handle to lower sternum, 1 sec squeeze
  • Full arm extension at the start
⚠ Common mistake
Rocking the torso to create momentum. Keep it still — all movement is arms and shoulder blades only.
3
Straight-arm cable pulldown
Isolates lats — no bicep involvement
3 × 12–15 Form
Form guide
Key cues
  • Cable high, stand back ~1m, hip hinge forward
  • Arms nearly straight — lock that angle
  • Pull bar to thighs using only lats
  • Squeeze at bottom, slow return to stretch
⚠ Common mistake
Bending the elbows mid-rep. The moment you do, biceps take over. Keep arms straight.
Rear delts
4
Pec deck reverse fly
Elbows slightly bent throughout
3 × 12–15 Form
Form guide
Key cues
  • Face machine, chest against pad
  • Drive elbows back — pull them behind you
  • Squeeze rear delts at peak, 1 sec hold
  • Return slowly, don't slam the weights
⚠ Common mistake
Straightening arms fully — turns it into a tricep move. Keep slight elbow bend locked in.
Biceps
5
EZ bar curl
Primary mass builder — slow eccentric
3 × 8–10 Form
Form guide
Key cues
  • Elbows pinned to sides — don't move
  • Curl up, supinate wrists at top
  • 3 sec controlled descent every rep
  • Full extension at the bottom
⚠ Common mistake
Swinging the torso back. That's your lower back working, not your biceps. Go lighter.
6
Hammer curl
Neutral grip throughout — no wrist rotation
2 × 10–12 Form
Form guide
Key cues
  • Neutral grip (palms face each other)
  • Elbows pinned to sides throughout
  • Thumbs point up at the top
  • Full extension at the bottom
⚠ Common mistake
Rotating the wrist into a supinated curl mid-rep. Keep neutral grip the whole way through.
Legs A
Wednesday
Quad focus — squat pattern leads
5 exercises · tap for form video
Quads — compound
1
Goblet squat → progress to hack squat
Go heavier each week, full depth
4 × 8–10 Form
Form guide
Key cues
  • Hold dumbbell at chest, elbows tucked under
  • Knees push out in toe direction as you descend
  • Sit between your heels, chest upright
  • Squeeze glutes hard at the top
⚠ Common mistake
Knees caving inward on the way up. Actively push knees out — "spread the floor with your feet".
2
Bulgarian split squat
Rear foot elevated, front knee tracks toe
3 × 10 each Form
Form guide
Key cues
  • Rear foot on bench (top of foot flat)
  • Drop hips straight down, not forward
  • Front knee tracks over 2nd/3rd toe
  • Drive through front heel to stand
⚠ Common mistake
Front foot too close — knee shoots past toes. Step further forward so your shin stays roughly vertical.
Posterior chain
3
Romanian deadlift (RDL)
Hip hinge — feel the hamstring stretch at bottom
3 × 8–10 Form
Form guide
Key cues
  • Soft knee bend, constant throughout
  • Push hips back — bar slides down the legs
  • Stop where back is flat (not where you feel like going)
  • Drive hips forward, squeeze glutes at top
⚠ Common mistake
Rounding the lower back chasing depth. Stop where you can maintain a flat back — the stretch is in the hamstrings, not the spine.
4
Leg curl (lying or seated)
3 sec eccentric — hamstrings grow here
3 × 10–12 Form
Form guide
Key cues
  • Hips flat on pad — don't let them rise
  • Curl to full contraction, squeeze 1 sec
  • 3 sec controlled descent every rep
  • Full extension at the bottom
⚠ Common mistake
Hips rising off the pad — glutes taking over. Drop the weight and keep hips pressed down.
Calves
5
Standing calf raise
Full ROM — pause at bottom stretch
4 × 12–15 Form
Form guide
Key cues
  • Balls of feet on edge, heels hanging
  • Lower heels fully — 2 sec pause at stretch
  • Rise as high as possible on toes
  • Don't bounce out of the bottom
⚠ Common mistake
Partial range — just bobbing. Calves need full stretch under load. Go all the way down every rep.
Rest 2–3 min after squats and RDL. These are your money sets — don't rush them.

Form guide

Form guide

2 × 12 Form
Form guide
Key cues
  • Raise to shoulder height only — not above
  • Nearly straight arms — slight elbow bend
  • No swinging — slow and controlled
⚠ Common mistake
Raising above shoulder height — that's traps, not front delts. Stop at shoulder level every rep.

Upper arms drifting forward. Lock them to your ribs and they stay there the entire set.

6 exercises · tap for form video
Back — thickness
1
Seated cable row (close grip / V-bar)
Lead compound — mid-back thickness
4 × 8–10 Form
Form guide
Key cues
  • Torso upright, lumbar arch maintained
  • Shoulder blades retract first, then pull
  • Handle to lower sternum, 1 sec peak squeeze
  • Full arm extension at the start
⚠ Common mistake
Torso swinging forward and back. Upper body stays still — all movement is arms and shoulder blades.
2
Single-arm dumbbell row
Full ROM — let shoulder blade travel
3 × 10 each Form
Form guide
Key cues
  • Row toward hip — not armpit
  • Elbow stays close to the body
  • Shoulder blade fully retracts at the top
  • Let blade protract at bottom — this is the full ROM
⚠ Common mistake
Rowing to the armpit with a flared elbow — that's rear delt work, not back. Elbow close, row to hip.
3
Cable face pull
Rear delts + external rotation — shoulder health
3 × 15 Form
Form guide
Key cues
  • Elbows stay HIGH — at shoulder height minimum
  • Pull to face, hands to sides of ears
  • Externally rotate at peak — forearms point up
  • Hold peak 1–2 sec — this is the whole point
⚠ Common mistake
Elbows dropping below shoulder height — turns it into a row. Elbows must stay high throughout.
Traps
4
Dumbbell shrug
Hold at top 1 second — straight up and down
3 × 12–15 Form
Form guide
Key cues
  • Straight up toward ears — no rolling
  • Hold peak for 1 full second
  • Lower slowly — feel the trap stretch
  • Arms stay straight throughout
⚠ Common mistake
Rolling the shoulders in circles. Traps move vertically only — straight up, straight down.
Biceps
5
Incline dumbbell curl
Long head stretch — elbows stay behind torso
3 × 10–12 Form
Form guide
Key cues
  • Arms hang behind torso — pre-stretches long head
  • Elbows stay behind torso — don't drift forward
  • Curl up, supinate at top, squeeze hard
  • Lower very slowly — the stretch is the point
⚠ Common mistake
Elbows drifting forward removes the incline advantage entirely. Keep them behind your torso throughout.
6
Cable curl (supinated)
Constant tension finisher
2 × 12–15 Form
Form guide
Key cues
  • Stand close to pulley — tension at bottom too
  • Supinated grip from start to finish
  • Elbows pinned, slow deliberate reps
  • Full extension at bottom — don't cut short
⚠ Common mistake
Standing too far from the cable — you lose tension at the bottom where cables are best. Stay close.

Quads — accessory
3
Leg press
Different knee angle from squat — quad variety
4 × 10–12 Form
Form guide
Key cues
  • Lower back and glutes pressed to seat throughout
  • Lower to 90° — don't let back peel off seat
  • Press through full foot — don't rise on toes
  • Don't lock knees at the top — keep tension
⚠ Common mistake
Fully locking the knees at the top and resting. Keep a slight bend — maintain quad tension the whole set.
4
Bulgarian split squat
Lighter than Legs A — focus on feel
2 × 10 each Form
Form guide
Key cues
  • All work through the front leg — rear is balance only
  • Drop straight down, rear knee nearly touches floor
  • Front knee tracks over 2nd/3rd toe
⚠ Common mistake
Pushing off the rear foot to help. All drive comes from the front leg only.
5
Leg extension
VMO isolation — slow controlled eccentric
3 × 12–15 Form
Form guide
Key cues
  • Knee joint aligned with machine pivot point
  • Full extension — squeeze quads hard, 1 sec hold
  • 3 sec descent — don't let weight drop
⚠ Common mistake
Jerking up and letting it drop. This is a pump exercise — slow and controlled is the whole point.
Calves
6
Seated calf raise
Hits soleus — different from standing raise
4 × 15 Form
Form guide
Key cues
  • Knee at 90° — activates soleus (deep calf)
  • Heels hang off — lower fully, 2 sec pause
  • Rise to full toe contraction
  • Don't bounce out of the bottom stretch
⚠ Common mistake
Skipping the bottom pause. The stretch under load is the stimulus — bouncing wastes the rep.
